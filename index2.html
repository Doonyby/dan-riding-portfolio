Demo/Source Back Country Ski Buddy Back Country Ski Buddy is a way to promote safe practices in Utah back country usage. Too many people use the back country alone which can be extremely dangerous when it comes to avalanche safety. Back Country Ski Buddy allows a user to find other people in their back country community with specific skill levels appropriate for a planned outing. This app was built using Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and Jquery.
